About Me

I am currently a Software Engineer Intern at Earnest Inc in San Francisco, building internal servicing tools with JavaScript, NodeJS, AngularJS, React, and PostgreSQL. I have experience working independently and in teams, using the agile development process. Check out my resume for more details.

As a web developer, I enjoy solving problems, working with creative, passionate people, and constantly learning new things. My favorite projects incorporate data, intuitive user experience, and easy access to information.

In my previous administrative and events management roles, I created systems that fostered efficient, clear communication; I looked at every detail of an event so the team was set up for success. I chose to pursue web development because I wanted a more challenging and fulfilling career where learning, innovation, and growth are encouraged at all levels.

I studied web development at General Assembly, where I built full-stack web applications in Ruby on Rails and postgreSQL, Javascript with Node, Express and MongoDB, and AngularJS.

When I'm not coding, I can be found volunteering with the Boys and Girls Clubs of San Francisco, cooking, hiking, traveling, and visiting art museums.
