About Me

I am a Software Engineer at Earnest Inc in San Francisco, building internal loan processing tools with JavaScript, NodeJS, AngularJS, React, and PostgreSQL. I have experience working independently and in teams, using the agile development process. Check out my resume for more details.

As an engineer, I enjoy solving problems, working with creative, passionate people, and constantly learning new things. My favorite projects incorporate data, intuitive user experience, and easy access to information.

In my previous administrative and events management roles, I created systems that fostered efficient, clear communication. I chose to pursue web development because I wanted a challenging and fulfilling career where learning, innovation, and growth are encouraged at all levels.

I studied web development at General Assembly, where I built full-stack web applications in Ruby on Rails and postgreSQL, Javascript with Node, Express and MongoDB, and AngularJS. I also worked there as a teaching assistant for a full-time web development course and a part-time front-end web development course.

When I'm not coding, I can be found volunteering as a mentor with Hackbright Academy (engineering bootcamp for women), hiking, traveling, and visiting art museums.
